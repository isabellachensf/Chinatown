Oakland Chinatown

My grandparents use to own a house in the outskirts of Chinatown so I spent most of my childhood there. My favorite memories are all the morning my grandma took me along with her to the park to play with my cousins while our grandma did tai chi. Then she would take me along with her friends to eat a nice filling breakfast at our OG favorite spots.
